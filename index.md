Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

DoseMate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the DoseMate mobile application (“App”).

By using DoseMate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide

When using DoseMate, you may voluntarily provide the following information:

Medication names, dosage, schedules, and notes

Health-related information related to medication management

Optional notes or reminders entered by the user

This information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical information to ensure app functionality and improve performance, including:

Device identifiers (such as an anonymous user ID)

App usage data (e.g., feature usage, crashes)

Device type, operating system version

This data does not include precise location or personal content outside the app.

1.3 Health Data

DoseMate may process health-related data, such as medication intake information, strictly for:

Medication reminders

Tracking adherence

Syncing data across your devices (if enabled)

Health data is handled with special care and is never sold or used for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate purposes, including:

Providing medication reminders and app features

Syncing data across devices using iCloud (if enabled)

Managing subscriptions and purchases

Improving app stability and performance

Responding to support requests

We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, or marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data.

However, DoseMate may use trusted third-party services to operate the app:

3.1 Apple Services

3.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases.

3.3 Analytics (If Enabled)

If analytics are enabled, we may collect anonymized usage data to improve app performance.
This data:

Is not used for advertising

Is not linked to your real-world identity

4. Data Tracking

We do not engage in cross-app tracking as defined by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.
