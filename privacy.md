# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## 1. Introduction
DoseMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

*   Medication names, dosages, and schedules
*   Prescription information and notes
*   Calendar synchronization data
*   Account information (email, if provided)
*   Usage data and analytics

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:

*   Provide, maintain, and improve the App
*   Send medication reminders and notifications
*   Synchronize your data across devices (if enabled)
*   Process subscriptions and payments
*   Respond to your requests and provide customer support
*   Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

## 4. Data Storage and Security
Your medication data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud synchronization features:

*   Data is encrypted during transmission
*   We use industry-standard security measures
*   You can delete your cloud data at any time

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services for:

*   Payment processing (Apple App Store)
*   Analytics and performance monitoring
*   Cloud storage (iCloud, Google Drive)
*   Calendar integration (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

## 6. Your Data Rights
You have the right to:

*   Access your personal data
*   Correct inaccurate data
*   Delete your data
*   Export your data
*   Opt-out of data collection where applicable

You can exercise these rights through the App settings or by contacting us.

## 7. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 8. International Data Transfers
Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## 10. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through:

*   The App's support section
*   Our official support channels

We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.
